A passionate advocate for anti-bullying initiatives, Veena Paveena represents the modern Thai woman with grace, intelligence, and purpose. As Miss Universe Thailand 2023, she continues to champion her "Be An Ally, Not A Bully" campaign, transforming lives across the nation.

Biography

Born: December 1, 1999, in Chiang Mai, Thailand

Education: Bachelor's Degree in Liberal Arts from Thammasat University

Hometown: Originally from Chiang Mai, currently based in Bangkok

Languages: Thai (Native), English (Fluent), Chinese (Conversational)

Special Talents: Traditional Thai Dance, Public Speaking, Youth Mentoring

Veena's journey began in the cultural heart of Northern Thailand, where traditional values and modern aspirations merged to shape her worldview. Her academic excellence at Thammasat University, combined with her natural charisma and deep empathy for others, positioned her as a natural leader and advocate.

Her passion for anti-bullying advocacy stems from personal experiences and a genuine desire to create safer spaces for young people. Through her platform as Miss Universe Thailand 2023, she has reached over 25,000 students across 150 schools nationwide.

Beyond pageantry, Veena is a certified youth counselor and has collaborated with educational institutions to develop comprehensive anti-bullying programs that focus on empathy, inclusion, and peer support systems.
