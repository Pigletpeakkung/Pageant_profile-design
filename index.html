Advocacy & Impact

Veena Paveena's anti-bullying campaign is at the heart of her mission. Through her "Safe Spaces" initiative, she has reached over 50,000 students across Thailand, equipping them with tools to combat bullying and foster empathy.

Key Program Components:

  • School Workshops: Interactive sessions on recognizing and preventing bullying.
  • Teacher Training: Equipping educators to handle bullying incidents effectively.
  • Peer Support Networks: Encouraging students to build supportive communities.
  • Digital Awareness: Addressing cyberbullying with online safety campaigns.

Her work has led to a reported 30% decrease in bullying incidents in partnered schools, and her social media campaigns have engaged over 1 million users with messages of kindness and inclusion.
